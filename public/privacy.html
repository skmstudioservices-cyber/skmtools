Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 March 2026

SKMTools ("we," "our," "us") is operated by SKM Studio Services. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using skmtools.pages.dev, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address: Collected when you request a report or register. We use this to send your report and referral information.
  • URL you submit: The website or Google Maps link you audit. We use this only to generate your audit results. We do not store this data beyond the session.
  • Country/region: Detected via your IP address's country code (Cloudflare CF-IPCountry header) to show relevant pricing. We do not store your IP address.
  • Referral code: If you came via a referral link, we log the referrer's code to credit them. Your identity is not shared with the referrer.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics (pages visited, audit count). No personal identifiers are stored in analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your audit report
  • To send your referral link and track referral rewards
  • To display correct pricing based on your country
  • To improve our audit tool and content
  • We do NOT sell your data to any third party
  • We do NOT share your email with advertisers

3. Disposable Email Policy

We block known disposable and temporary email domains to prevent abuse of our referral system. If your email is blocked incorrectly, contact us at the address below.

4. Referral Programme

When you share your referral link, others who click it will be registered with your referral code to track qualifying referrals. We do not send your referral contacts anything — the sharing is initiated entirely by you.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email and referral data for up to 2 years or until you request deletion. Audit logs are retained for 90 days for analysis purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your country, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@skmstudio.in. We will respond within 30 days.

GDPR (EU/UK): You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or port your data. You have the right to object to processing. Our legal basis for processing email data is your explicit consent at signup.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies — only those necessary for the site to function (e.g. session state). We do not set advertising or tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics by default.

8. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare: Our CDN and hosting provider. Cloudflare may log request metadata. Cloudflare Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Used for typography. Google may receive your IP address when loading fonts.

9. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will post the updated date at the top of this page. Continued use after changes means acceptance of the new policy.

11. Contact
